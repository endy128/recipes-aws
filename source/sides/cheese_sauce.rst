Cheese Sauce
============

**Serves 2**

Ingredients
-----------

===== =========================
50g   Grated cheese
25ml  Beer / milk
2.5g  Sodium citrate (or less)
===== =========================


.. note::

  Sodium citrate can be **maximum** 5% of the dish


Method
------

1. Heat beer and mix sodium citrate well
2. Add cheese and combine.
