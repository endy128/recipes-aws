Cheese Sauce

Serves 2

Ingredients

50g

Grated cheese

25ml

Beer / milk

2.5g

Sodium citrate (or less)

Note

Sodium citrate can be maximum 5% of the dish

Method

  1. Heat bear and mix sodium citrate well

  2. Add cheese and combine.
